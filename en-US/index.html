Introducing Druid.

Need reasons to choose druid? Look no further.

By nerds, for nerds.

Built at Alibaba by wenshao, Druid is no dependency with other jars, is compiled via Maven, and is managed through GitHub to help nerds do awesome stuff on the jdbc connection pool.

Made for everyone.

Druid was made to not only jdbc connection pool, but a built-in monitor service as well. see Druid monitor

Packed with features.

A monitor service Druid monitor, SQL Parser, Proxy Druid can be used by your own.

What Druid focus.

Focus, Do not be distracted.

Druid focus on JDBC connection pool, and help developer monitor JDBC connection to maintain RDBMS related program.
